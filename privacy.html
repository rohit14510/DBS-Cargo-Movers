Privacy Policy

At DBS Cargo Movers, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information:We collect personal details such as your name, address, email, and phone number when you request a quote or use our services.
  • Service Information:Information related to the services you use, including details about your move, such as inventory, pick-up, and delivery locations.
  • Payment Information: Payment details necessary for processing transactions, including credit card numbers and billing addresses.

How We Use Your Informationt

  • Service Delivery: To provide and manage our packing and moving services, including scheduling, transportation, and customer support.
  • Communication: To contact you with updates, respond to inquiries, and provide information about our services.
  • Payment Processing: To process payments for the services you receive.
  • Improvement: To improve our services based on feedback and usage patterns.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors and partners who assist us in providing our services, such as transportation and payment processing.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights, comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Our data protection practices are regularly reviewed to ensure your information remains secure.

Your Choices

You can choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit your ability to use some of our services. You can also opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those messages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes by email or through our website.
